Composite Veneers

Direct veneers, also known as Composite Veneers, are made with a tooth-coloured filling material. They are used to improve the appearance of discoloured and damaged teeth that are misaligned or have unattractive gaps between them. Placing these veneers does not require drilling or injections and instantly transforms smiles. In substance, they are a layer of a resin material that is applied to the teeth directly. Sometimes called instant or Resin Veneers, they can be placed in just one visit – leaving the patient with a gorgeous smile in just a few hours.
