Fluorosis

Are you unhappy that you have fluorosis? It is a common dental condition affecting many people and leads to patchy and mottled discoloured spots on the teeth. Even though many cases are mild, fluorosis can often get severe – having a considerable impact on the appearance of teeth and ruining a sparkly smile. Unfortunately, teeth whitening treatments can worsen the condition by highlighting the contrasted discoloured spots on the teeth. If you are suffering from fluorosis, take charge of the situation and invest in getting veneers. They cover the surface of the teeth and hide the appearance of spots.
